  • About the TrimWorks Family

    Serving Tampa Bay Since 2005 - Licensed and Insured

    Trimworks has been providing high quality tree care since 2005. We are centrally located to service all of the Central and Southwest Regions of Florida, including Pinellas, Hillsborough, Pasco, Hernando, Lee and Collier Counties. We specialize in large and hazardous tree removals, large scale tree/palm tree trimming (golf courses, mobile home parks, RV parks, apartment complexes) and tree health evaluations. We also have a 24 hour Storm Response Team ready for when Mother Nature calls.

    Trimworks offers a vast array of services including: Tree Removals, Tree Pruning, Tree Planting and Recommendations, Stump Grinding, Tree and Plant relocation, Root Pruning, Lightning Protection, Free Tree Evaluations, Mulching services, Mangrove Trimming, Lot Clearing/yard cleanouts and Crane Services.

    Our tree division is dedicated to educating customers on the importance of trees and how proper tree care is essential to tree health. With knowledgeable ISA Certified Arborists on staff we can assist you every step of the way. Our landscape division is educated in taking a more native and natural approach in our landscape designs. We believe planting the right tree, in the right place is a vital factor when designing and planning a landscape.

    Trimworks is equipped to get the job done efficiently and safely. Our crew takes their time and takes pride in the services they provide to our clients. They are properly trained on the safe and correct way to use their equipment and gear. Our staff always wear the required safety gear and are always in uniform. Trimworks employees are CPR and First-Aid certified and receive regular training in Aerial Rescue Techniques and Applications. They are professional, approachable and knowledgeable about our industry. These safety measures are important in our industry to help minimize the risk of property damage or injury. Trimworks carries comprehensive General Liability and Workers’ compensation policies. In the event of an accident, you are in safe hands with us.

  • Meet the Owner

    Vern Gingras is an ISA Certified Arborist (FL-9182A).  He has dedicated the last decade to growing Trimworks into the multi divisional company it is today. He is very knowledgeable about the industry and strives to educate himself, his employees and his customers every day.  You may see him in a collar shirt giving estimates or getting down and dirty with the guys.  He is a hands on boss, and works with the crews to maintain the same reliable and affordable services our customers have grown to know and love. Vern is kind, funny and honest. He has an old school approach when it comes to his company "Be true to your word, take pride in your work, and encourage others to be the best they can be." He treats his staff with respect and in return expects that they will treat every customer as if they are family.  Vern truly cares about his crew and knows that Trimworks would not be where it is today without the support and dedication of our employees and clients.
